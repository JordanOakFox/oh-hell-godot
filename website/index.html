Rules

How Oh Hell works.

The goal is to predict exactly how many tricks you will win each round.

1. Deal and trump

  • Each round deals a set number of cards.
  • After dealing, the next card from the deck sets trump.
  • Trump cards beat non-trump cards.

2. Secret bidding

  • Everyone bids how many tricks they think they can win.
  • Bids are hidden until all players lock in.
  • Your goal is to match your bid exactly.

3. Playing tricks

  • The leader plays any card.
  • Everyone else must follow suit if they can.
  • The trick winner leads the next trick.

4. Scoring

  • If you make your bid, you score points.
  • If you miss your bid, you do not get the bid bonus.
  • Highest score at the end wins.

Table controls

  • Press Tab to toggle the scoreboard.
  • Press M to mute or unmute audio.
  • Right-click and drag to look around the table.
